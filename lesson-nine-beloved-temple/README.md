## Translation
For his lord Nanna, the eldest son of Enlil,  Ur-Nammu
-- the mighty man, the lord of Uruk, the king of Ur, the king of Sumer and Akkad --
built Etemenniguru, his beloved temple. He restored it to its former glory.
